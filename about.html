ABOUT US

Who are WE?


The enviroment that we are living in is getting devastated day by day. We are the youth of Kosova, who are trying to make a change in the enviroment that we are already living in.

How are WE planning to do this change?


Within every week in the voting page there will be 4 most polluted locations that will be chosen wisely by our commission, and then after the voters select one out of these 4 locations, our team will present the location to the relevant institutions of that location, and hopefully take action.

Why are WE doing this?

One little step from us, can be a big step for our future ones.
